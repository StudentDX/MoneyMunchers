About / Frequently Asked Questions



PepperMint is a personal finance system designed to help you keep track of your money more easily. We remind you when you are approaching your budgets to help curb your spending.
When you go into your profile, if you haven't already set your budget there will be two fields. The first asks for your daily budget, and the second asks for your monthly budget. Once you submit these fields, you'll have set your budgets. You can also change your budget once submitted.
To view more information about your expenses, just click on that expense in your profile page. This will bring up more information about each expense. If you would like to view more about your spending habits, go to the Trends page.
PepperMint is 100% free!
